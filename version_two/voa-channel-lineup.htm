Channel Line-up – Voice Of The Agents

Please perform the following activities at least two hours before the VOA.

Updates: We've listened to agent feedback and added two ways to make searching for your customer's favorite channels faster.

  1. Please open the VOA version of Channel Line-up.
  2. Search for a channel (by name, number, call letters, or genre)
    • As you type the channel list will filter channels based on your search term
    • There are now two ways to clear the search field for your next search
    • You can click the "Reset" button which should now be active (red background), or press the Escape key, however this will clear your previous search results
    • Pressing Enter will keep your search results intact but prepare the search field for your next search term. You will also be given a message to let you know what the search term was (in case of misspelling/unexpected results)
  3. Search for several search terms, separated by a comma
    (ex: "disney,331,HIST" or "disney, 331, HIST" – space after the comma is optional)
    • Your results will include the sum of all search terms
    • This will allow you to see which packages contain the channels at a glance
  4. OPTIONAL: After familiarizing yourself with the new functionality, try using this version of Channel Line-up on a live call.
    • There should be no difference in performance between this VOA version and the production version of the tool.
    • Please note any discrepancies, however, should they arise.
